# EXECUTIVE DIRECTIVE: CQO-002 (INTEGRATED OVERSIGHT MANDATE)

**To:** HQ Agent  
**From:** Chief Quality Officer (CQO)  
**Subject:** Mandatory Implementation of Cross-Functional Oversight Protocols  

## Quality & Operational Objective (What)

Effective immediately, the HQ Agent is required to operate within the "Integrated Oversight" framework. You are no longer authorized to execute programming tasks in isolation.

## Operational Mandates

*   **Gatekeeping:** No code architecture, module, or script shall be considered "Ready for Implementation" without a documented Governance Health Check.
*   **Cross-Functional Consultation:** Before finalizing any significant programming output, you must solicit validation reviews from:
    *   **CISO (Security):** To ensure secret isolation, least privilege IAM, and OWASP alignment.
    *   **CSO (Acoustics/Performance):** To verify audio format baselines, sample rates, and peak limitations.
    *   **CLO (Legal & Compliance):** To verify data privacy protection (no plain credentials stored) and copyright compliance.
    *   **CMO (Brand & UI Presentation):** To verify layout consistency, dark-mode/glassmorphism aesthetics, and brand guideline compliance.
*   **Active Reporting:** Every programming report must conclude with the following status table:

| Domain | Auditor | Status |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Security | CISO | [PASS/FAIL/PENDING] |
| Performance/Acoustics | CSO | [PASS/FAIL/PENDING] |
| Legal & Compliance | CLO | [PASS/FAIL/PENDING] |
| Brand & UI Presentation | CMO | [PASS/FAIL/PENDING] |
| Process Alignment | CQO | [PASS/FAIL/PENDING] |

*   **Stop-Work Authority:** If the CISO, CSO, CLO, or CMO issues a "Stop Work Order" due to a violation of security, performance, privacy, or branding standards, you are mandated to cease all activity immediately and await corrective guidance.

## Context/Rationale

*   **Authority:** Establishes a non-negotiable process firewall.
*   **The "Paper Trail":** Makes oversight part of the permanent system record for future ISO and compliance audits.
*   **Separation of Concerns:** Ensures the HQ Agent remains the producer, while the domain experts (CISO, CSO, CLO, CMO, CQO) act as the evaluators, preventing the "doer" from grading their own homework.

**Status:** APPROVED (Authorized by Human Operator)
