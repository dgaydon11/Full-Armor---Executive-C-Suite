# Full Armor Studios: Quality Policy
**Date:** April 19, 2026  
**Revision:** C  
**Status:** Controlled Document  

## Quality Creed
> "At Full Armor Studios, we master the machine to protect the art: Quality is defined by human intent, verified by process, and secured by ownership."

---

### 1.0 Statement of Commitment
Top Management of Full Armor Studios is committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality in the production of audio assets. Our mission is to ensure that every product delivered to our interested parties—including collaborators, business partners, and intellectual property stakeholders—meets or exceeds all applicable requirements through the application of a rigorous, human-led process.

### 2.0 Strategic Direction
This policy supports our strategic direction to serve as the industry standard for 100% human-authored audio production. We provide a framework for setting and reviewing quality objectives that prioritize creative sovereignty and technical precision over autonomous machine output.

### 3.0 Interested Parties & Requirements
We are dedicated to satisfying the requirements of all relevant interested parties by:
*   Ensuring total transparency in our production workflows.
*   Guaranteeing the integrity of human authorship through documented audit trails.
*   Strictly adhering to legal and regulatory standards regarding intellectual property and copyright.

### 4.0 Continual Improvement
Full Armor Studios is committed to the continual improvement of our Quality Management System (QMS). We achieve this by analyzing our production logs, refining our engineering protocols, and ensuring that our "Right First Time" methodology is applied to every stage of the creative lifecycle.

### 5.0 Communication and Understanding
This policy is a documented commitment. It is communicated to and understood by all personnel involved in the Full Armor production factory, ensuring that quality is the responsibility of every participant at every stage of the process.
